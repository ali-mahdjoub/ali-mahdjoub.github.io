✨ Travel & Tourism App
Android
Java

Travel & Tourism App is a full-featured Android mobile application I developed from scratch to digitalize both client services and internal operations for a modern travel agency. This multifunctional app integrates booking, invoicing, HR, finance, maps, and communication into one seamless mobile experience.

✨ Key Features
  • Authentication & User Management – Login/signup, profile updates (name, email, DOB, password)
  • Clients & Services Modules – Manage individual and corporate clients, services, filtering, statistics & graphs
  • Sales & Invoicing – Track sales, generate invoices (PDF & Excel), view trends and comparisons
  • Finance Management – Monitor salaries, expenses, and costs with financial charts and Excel export
  • HR & Task Management – Employee profiles, task assignments, lifecycle tracking and planning
  • Relations & Public Data Modules – Supplier, public relations, embassy databases, and event calendars
  • Geolocation & Maps – Locate travel agencies using Google Maps API with satellite view
  • Email Automation – Integrated SMTP for automatic email sending (booking confirmations, invoices, etc.)
